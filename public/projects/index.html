Mapping Psychedelic Rock

Janis Joplin & Jefferson Airplane: 1966-1970

Created alongside a project proposal for HIST 8540 - Spatial History, this map displays the locations of Janis Joplin and Jefferson Airplane's concerts over 1966-1970.
Each year is separated into a separate layer - able to be toggled on and off to hide/reveal overlapping venues.
